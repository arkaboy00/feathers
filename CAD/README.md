## Requirements

- The CAD files are provided in **.m3d format** (КОМПАС-3D).
- They were created in the **educational (non-commercial) version** of КОМПАС-3D.
- To open and edit them, you will need the same educational version, or the Home/Teaching version. They are **not compatible** with standard commercial licenses of КОМПАС-3D.

For convenience, ready-to-print **.stl** files are also provided in the `/STL` folder.
